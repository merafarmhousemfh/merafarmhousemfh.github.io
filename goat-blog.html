Goat farming is an extremely profitable and sustainable business in India. It is easy and simple farming that can help you earn good income. If you are interested in Goat farming or if you already have goat farming setup but want to increase your business or want to know about how to start organic farming in home then this blog is for you. To get answers to all these questions Mera Farmhouse team went to Sainsowal, Una, Himachal Pradesh to meet Baba Bikriya Wala.


Unique goat farm setup

When we reached here, and we found a unique farm setup here for goat farming. The goats are kept in a shed with fencing all around. They told us that goats eat upto 4kg of feed and drinks a lot of water. In feed the goat prefers to eat fresh leaves.


No smell from the milk

People usually say that goat milk is smelly but they told us that goats milk produced here is smell free. The reason for it is that goats here go for grassing in the forest nearby and eats only fresh leaves. Everything produced from goats like milk, it‚Äôs waste and even urine can be sold. So, goat farming is highly profitable business.


How to take care of goats?

If you want to generate good income from goat farming then you have to make sure that goats are healthy. You need to get time to time consultations from animal husbandry department and immediate treatment for the disease in goats. Major problem in goats is loose motion. As goats graze in the field so it is common for goat to get stomach diseases. Veterinary doctor in your area can help you treat your goat. Fencing is done to keep the goats in protected area.


Washbasins are used for water supply

Water is provided through wash basins to prevent diseases spread. Mostly goats get disease due to their ear gets water when they drink water. Here washbasins are provided where they can easily drink water without getting wet. 24*7 water supply is provided to the goats.


Shahen shah goat breeder- best goat breeder in the area

They have one goat breeder - shahen shah. It is a famous breeder found only in their farm. People from nearby comes here to breed their goats. Generally breeding is done after 150 days and there are 100% chances the goat gets breaded, in case if it doesn‚Äôt then there can be some health problem with the goat. Even injection for breeding is in process of making to breed goats. The mating of goats can be done after 45 days of first breeding.


How sheds are prepared for goats?

He told us that one goat requires 4*4=16 sqft area and he has 50*15=750 sqft farm where he has 65 goats. Goat is a calm animal and gives an income of 10-15k in a year. The best part of goat farming is that unlike other animals like cow or buffalo they don‚Äôt have fix milking time, you can milk it anytime. Normally, goats give 200 to 250 g of milk and 1.5 to 2 kg of milk in a day. It is also known as milk ATM.


What feed is given to a goat?

Goats are choosy animals and eat food of its own choice and generally prefers to feed from forest. In the farm morning feed is given to the goats in which they prepare food at 7 a.m. The morning feed include maize, oats, moil, corn, barley etc. and then goes for grazing at 9 a.m. They normally feed for 2 to 2:30 hrs.


Breeds available in the farm

They have Beetal goat breed in farm. The adult buck has 50-60kg weight and adult doe has 35-40kg weight. The body length of male beetal is approximately 86cm and that of female beetal is approximately 71cm. They have long legs, long and pendulous ears, short and thin tail and have backward curved horns. Their ears are tagged by the animal husbandry department. Different farms sell milk at different price but here he sells milk at Rs.150 per liter.


Is there any subsidy available for goat farming?

Government of Haryana, Punjab and Himachal Pradesh provide subsidy for goat farming. Haryana government provides upto 90% of subsidy. The subsidy available depends on the fencing and the number of goats. You can also consult your veterinary doctor to get complete information about the current available subsidies.


It is very simple farming unlike buffalos that cost upto 80k. Goat are easily available at 15k and even you can get 2k when dead. A goat can give upto 2 kids, in 4 to 5 months you can sell the kid and earn upto 20k. A goat gives return of its price in almost 1.5 years. This farming business can give you profit if you keep the goat healthy. As 90% diseases in goats are caused due to the foot. You need to keep cleanliness in the farm to prevent the spreading of diseases.
