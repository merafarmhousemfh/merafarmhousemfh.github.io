Location-based Attendance

Instructors/teachers set a geofence around a location on a map where the class is held.

Self Check-in for Participants

Participants/students use a mobile app to do a self check-in to record their attendance.

Reporting & Analytics for Instructors

Instructors/teachers can view attendance reports and analytics in the dashboard.

Solution

Mera Farmhouse is an advanced location-based attendance tracking solution and can be used within universities, social events, concerts, or at corporations when ensuring remote or on-campus work policies relating to physical presence at a location and a time interval are complied with.
